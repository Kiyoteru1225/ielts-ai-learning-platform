## Variant: Notion Warm

### Design stance
Notion's warm minimalism applied to IELTS writing practice — approachable, paper-like, low-pressure learning environment.

### Key choices
- **Layout**: Single-column centered (900px max), generous vertical rhythm
- **Typography**: Inter font, compressed letter-spacing at headings (-1.125px at 40px), 16px body
- **Color**: Notion warm white (#f6f5f4) background, pure white cards, whisper borders (1px rgba(0,0,0,0.1))
- **Interaction**: Pill tabs for task type, real-time word counter, 1.2s simulated AI scoring delay

### Trade-offs
- Strong at: Calm, focused reading/writing experience; reduced cognitive load for stressed test-takers
- Weak at: Not flashy; no dark mode; minimal visual excitement

### Best for
- Chinese IELTS students who want a distraction-free, "notebook-like" writing environment
- Long writing sessions where visual fatigue is a concern
